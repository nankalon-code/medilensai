# MediLens — Understand Your Health Reports

MediLens is an AI-powered tool that translates complex medical reports into clear, actionable health insights. Built with privacy at its core, it helps users understand lab results, track health trends, and make informed decisions.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Plain-Language Insights** – Converts medical jargon into easy-to-understand explanations.
- **Privacy-First Design** – Zero-knowledge architecture with on-device processing. No data stored.
- **Multi-Language Support** – Parses reports in 40+ languages including English, Hindi, Arabic, Mandarin, and Spanish.
- **Cross-Report Intelligence** – Tracks metrics over time to reveal trends and risk patterns.
- **50+ Report Types** – Supports PDFs, images, and text from labs, prescriptions, and more.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Upload** – Drag & drop any medical report (PDF, image, or text).
2. **Analyze** – AI interprets metrics against medical reference ranges.
3. **Understand** – Receive clear explanations, risk flags, and recommendations.

## 🛡️ Built With Privacy

- All processing happens in ephemeral containers that self-destruct after analysis.
- End-to-end encryption ensures your data never leaves your device.
- No data is stored — we literally cannot see your results.
